DESCRIPTION
function HCL67 will calculate the specific enthalpy and entropy of water at pressure P and temp T. 

    DEFINITION AND PURPOSE:
          o H,S=f(P,T)
          o Calculates specific enthalpy and specific entropy


    REGION OF APPLICATION:
